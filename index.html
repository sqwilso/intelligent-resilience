About This Practice

"Resilience means the ability to absorb disruption and keep going — whatever its source. Intelligent Resilience works at the intersection of cyber security and AI governance, helping organisations master both the threats coming in and the complexity they are building inside."

Over 20+ years leading senior technology and digital transformation engagements, I have built strategic frameworks and operating models that help organisations move from reactive, fragmented cyber security to intelligent, resilient operations.

The emergence of AI copilots and autonomous agents has added a new dimension to that work. Traditional security tools were built to detect threats from outside. They cannot see what AI systems are doing inside your organisation — the apps your teams are building, the data those systems are touching, or the ways they can be turned against you. That is the gap this practice was built to address.
