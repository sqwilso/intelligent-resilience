Agent Security Services

Audit, govern, and respond to AI agents in production. Help enterprises secure their autonomous agents before they cause problems.

The Challenge

Organizations are rapidly deploying AI agents in critical business functions—financial decisions, healthcare recommendations, HR hiring, supply chain operations. But enterprise security hasn't kept pace.

Traditional cybersecurity tools (EDR, SIEM, firewalls) detect external attackers. They cannot detect or respond to agent misbehavior—the new class of risk where agents exhibit emergent, unpredictable behavior that isn't malicious attack, but causes real business harm.

Without proper assessment and governance, enterprises face: compliance violations, unfair decisions, boundary violations, unpredictable behavior in production.

Our Service

We provide Agent Security Advisory Services to enterprises deploying AI agents in critical business functions. Our engagement includes:

  • Agent Security Assessment: Audit your agents in production, establish baseline behavior patterns, identify security and compliance risks, and design incident response procedures.
  • Governance Framework Design: Help you develop policies for agent deployment, approval procedures, escalation matrices, and compliance validation.
  • Regulatory Alignment: Ensure your agents comply with applicable regulations (GDPR, FCRA, Equal Opportunity, SOX, HIPAA).
  • Incident Response Support: Expert guidance when agents misbehave or produce unexpected results.
  • Bias & Fairness Review: Statistical analysis of agent decisions to identify and mitigate bias.

How We Work: Every engagement is custom-scoped based on your specific agents, compliance requirements, and risk tolerance. We work with your team to understand what you're trying to achieve, what risks you're concerned about, and what governance structure makes sense for your organization.

We integrate with your existing monitoring platform (Zenity, Astrix, Microsoft Agent 365, or custom solutions) and provide the expertise layer on top of your tool stack.

FAQ

Do I need monitoring in place first?

Not necessarily. If you have agents in production but no monitoring, we'll help you evaluate and deploy appropriate monitoring solutions. If you already have monitoring in place, we integrate with your existing platform.

What does engagement look like?

Every engagement is custom-scoped based on your specific situation. We start with a discovery conversation to understand your agents, compliance requirements, and risk concerns. From there, we recommend the right advisory approach for your organization.

What compliance regulations do you cover?

GDPR, FCRA (credit decisions), Equal Opportunity (hiring), SOX (financial), HIPAA (healthcare), and others depending on your industry. We assess your agents against applicable regulatory requirements and help you establish controls and evidence for audit.

Who on my team should be involved?

Ideally: Chief Risk Officer or CISO, the team that deployed or manages the agents, compliance officer, and executive sponsor. We'll work with whoever is available and help you identify the right stakeholders.

Is this the same as a security audit?

It's similar in approach but specialized for AI agents. We're not conducting a traditional security audit; we're helping you understand how to govern autonomous agents specifically, which is a different discipline than traditional cybersecurity.
